### Historical Context

- His order requires verification via "print reporters or traditional legal databases"
- This sparked a wave of similar orders across federal courts

### Patterns Observed
1. **Federal courts** tend to have individual judge standing orders rather than district-wide rules
2. **State supreme courts** are forming committees and issuing guidance (RI, VT) rather than binding rules
3. **Disclosure requirements** are the most common category (disclosure_required)
4. Many states have NO court-specific AI policies yet, relying on existing ethics rules
